## 🗣️ Interview Prep Topics

### Technical Questions (expect these)
- [ ] Design a CI/CD pipeline for microservices
- [ ] How would you implement GitOps for 4 environments?
- [ ] Explain your Terraform state management strategy
- [ ] How do you handle secrets in Kubernetes?
- [ ] Describe your monitoring/alerting approach
- [ ] How would you do zero-downtime deployments?

- [ ] How do you handle drift detection in Terraform?

- [ ] Tell me about a time you stabilized a failing platform
